About Our Club

Inspired by the values of St. John Baptist de La Salle, we work to identify and support less privileged students and community members.

Our Mission

The Lasallian Club at St. Joseph Catholic School – Adama is dedicated to spreading the spirit of sharing and supporting those in need. We identify less privileged students and members of the local community who require assistance and facilitate the necessary support through fundraising, sponsorships, and donations.

Our Values

Compassion

Showing care and understanding for those in need

Service

Dedicated to helping others selflessly

Community

Building strong relationships and support networks

Faith

Guided by Lasallian principles and Catholic values

Our Activities & Events

Throughout the year, we organize various initiatives to support our community and raise funds for those in need.

Fundraising Events

We organize various fundraising activities including bake sales, charity walks, and cultural events to gather resources for our initiatives.

Community Outreach

Regular visits to local communities to identify needs, distribute supplies, and provide support to families and individuals.

Student Support Programs

Providing educational materials, uniforms, and mentorship to less privileged students within our school and neighboring institutions.
