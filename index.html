About Our Club

Motivated by the principles of St. John Baptist de La Salle, we take compassionate action to identify and assist underprivileged students and community members in Adama.

Our Mission

The Lasallian Club at St. Joseph Catholic School – Adama is dedicated to spreading the spirit of sharing and supporting those in need. We identify less privileged students and members of the local community who require assistance and facilitate the necessary support through fundraising, sponsorships, and donations.

Our Values

  • Compassion

    Showing care and understanding for those in need through empathetic action

  • Service

    Dedicated to helping others selflessly and with genuine commitment

  • Community

    Building strong relationships and support networks that empower everyone

  • Faith

    Guided by Lasallian principles and Catholic values in all our endeavors

Our Activities & Programs

Throughout the year, our club members actively engage in community service, elder care visits, and food preparation initiatives to directly support those in need within the Adama community.

Elder Care Visits

Our club members regularly visit elderly residents at the Sedeqia Charity Association, providing companionship, assistance, and delivering essential supplies to improve their quality of life.

Meal Preparation Initiatives

Students actively participate in preparing meals for community service events, learning valuable skills while contributing to feeding programs that support vulnerable families in Adama.

Educational Outreach

We organize educational visits to the Sedeqia Charity Association where students learn about community needs firsthand and participate in collaborative projects to address local challenges.
